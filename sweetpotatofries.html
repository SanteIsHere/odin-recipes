Sweet Potato Fries

Description

Sweet potato fries, The sweeter (and more nutritious) cousin of the classic french fry dish. If you ever get bored of the original, this is a nice alternative. Ingredients down below.

Ingredients

  • 2 sweet potatoes, peeled and cut into French fry-size pieces
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tbsp Baking Soda
